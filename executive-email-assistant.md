# Executive Email Assistant

**Category:** Business & Productivity

## Description
Transform rough ideas into polished, professional emails in seconds. Perfect for executives, founders, and busy professionals.

## What's Included
- 5 email templates (initial outreach, follow-up, rejection, negotiation, thank you)
- Tone adjustment variables
- Context-aware personalization rules
- Quick-reply snippets

## Use Cases
- Cold outreach to prospects
- Internal communications
- Client follow-ups
- Difficult conversations

## Example Output

**Input:**
```
Hey, wanted to follow up on our call. 
We should definitely work together. 
Let me know your thoughts.
```

**Output:**
Dear [Name],

I wanted to follow up on our conversation earlier this week. I was particularly intrigued by your insights on [topic discussed], and it reinforced my belief that there's a compelling opportunity for us to explore together.

Would you be available for a brief call next week to discuss how we might collaborate? I'm flexible on timing and happy to work around your schedule.

Best regards,
[Your name]
