# Meeting Notes to Action Items

**Category:** Business Productivity

## Description
Transform messy meeting notes into clear, actionable next steps with owners and deadlines.

## What's Included
- Meeting summary generator
- Action item extractor
- Owner assignment helper
- Deadline suggestion engine
- Follow-up reminder generator
- Priority classifier
